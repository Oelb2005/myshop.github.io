FAQ’s

What is My shop platform ?
It is a solution for every person or business owner wants to start e-commerce by getting his own website and applications for (Android, IOS) devices with his logo and brand identity, and he shall manage orders and products through his own control panel.

How can I register on My shop platform ?
You can apply through the link (mapp.sa/register) or communicate via WhatsApp (https://wa.me/966564207393) .

How long does it take to get my website and e-store App ?
You can create the site within minutes and the time it takes to do the applications is 7 working days starting from the date of subscription to the package.

What are the costs of getting my own e-store ?
See the packages page here

What are working times ?
Opening times from Sunday to Thursday. from 8 am to 5 pm.

Does My shop platform own a legal entity in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia ?
Yes

Is it required to have a commercial registry to register with the My shop platform ?
No, the commercial register is not required for the store unless you want to link the store with payment gateways to provide payment services such as Mada, Visa, MasterCard, and it is preferable to have it to preserve your identity and approval and to increase the reliability of customers for your store in case you need help in opening a commercial registry, steps to open a commercial registry.

Does the store include logo design and business identity ?
The subscription does not include obtaining a design for the logo and identity and this service can be provided separately with a special agreement.

Does obtaining a store include data entry for the e-store ?
The subscription does not include entering product data in the store and this service can be provided separately with a special agreement.

Does obtaining a store include photographing products of the owner’s e-store ?
The subscription does not include the production of imaging of the store products and this service can be provided separately with a special agreement.

Does the store that I have will support e-payment via Mada, Visa and MasterCard cards ?
Yes and you can activate it through the control panel.

Is the store I will get linked to shipping and delivery methods ?
Yes and you can benefit from it through the control panel.

Do you provide the store owner with the products that he displays in his store ?
No, every merchant is the one who provides his own products that he wants to display to sell in the e-store.

Do you provide e-marketing service on My shop platform for merchants ?
We are not currently providing e-marketing, but we are working on finding a partner to offer this service to merchants

Do My shop charge a commission on the store's e-payments ?
No, the Commission identified is for the e-payment method provided for the service.

Are the funds of the store sales transferred to My shop account ?
No, all the money is paid directly to the e-store and My shop has nothing to do with the sales and profits of the merchant.
